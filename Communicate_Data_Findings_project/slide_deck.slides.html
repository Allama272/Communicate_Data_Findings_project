Effect of Loan's Characteristics on Their Annual Interest Rate¶

by Abdullah Allama¶

Investigation Overview¶

The goal of this analysis is to tell which Characteristics of a loan is best fit predicing its annual interest rate.

Dataset Overview¶

The data consists of 80 attributes of approximately 114,000 loans. The attributes include ProsperScore, Occupation, IncomeRange, Term, and others.

Prosper Score vs Annual Interest Rate¶

By ploting the average Annual Interest Rate for every prosper score, it is clear that the higher the prosper score is the lower the annual interest rate will be.
